Metashape f Calculator

Convert focal length, sensor size, and image dimensions into Metashape's pixel-unit focal length and pixel size values.

Metashape calibration uses focal length in pixels. When your focal length is in millimeters:
pixel_size_mm = sensor_width_mm / image_width_px
metashape_f_px = focal_length_mm / pixel_size_mm
